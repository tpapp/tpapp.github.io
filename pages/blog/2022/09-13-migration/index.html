Migration from Hugo to Franklin.jl

2022-09-13 announcement

After a long break, I am restarting my blog. In addition to personal reasons, the main reason I did not keep it up is that I found Hugo a bit difficult to work with: updates to Hugo required updates to my setup, and integration with Julia was less than ideal. I have now switched to Franklin.jl.

I do not have the time to convert all my earlier posts, they remain available in this directory. I hope that this will keep old links working, if there is a problem please let me know and I will try to fix it.
